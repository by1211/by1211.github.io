Profile

My name is Chinh, I'm a designer based in Sydney who specializes in communication and interactivity production. Computer graphic, still or otherwise, is my biggest passion and have always been the centrality for all my creative process and craftmanship. My workflow is highly adaptable within agile developement context, though I also excel in working independently.

On the occasion when I'm not nudging pixels, my time is spent on exploring local food scene, runnng, playing video game or doing volunteering service. Like most millenials, I'm addicted to memes, dad jokes and coffee.
