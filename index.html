Food Giving

Food Giving is a concept born from a hackathon back in December. The idea is to build a platform where people can give away or trade grocery, things that they have no need for but is fresh.

There are 2 components built for this project. The landing page which serves as an information gateway to promote our application, displaying news, media, site policy and disclaimer. The web application prototype demonstrates our vision for how the application should work on user's hand. The main focus here is the intuitive and friendly interface design which can adapt and change depends on user's need.
