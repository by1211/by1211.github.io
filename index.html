About

My name is Chinh, I'm a designer based in Sydney. Computer graphic (still or otherwise) is my biggest passion, so I've spent a good part of training on learning and creating website, layout, illustration and logo. I'm also a team player whom everyone can count on to deliver top-notch quality design and occasional quirky drawings.

On the occasion when I'm not nudging pixels, my time is split among restaurant scouting, jogging, playing games and volunteering at local community. A cup of coffee is my go-to every morning.
